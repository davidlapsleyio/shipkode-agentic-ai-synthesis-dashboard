# Identification of Emerging Market Patterns

As an Opportunity Hunter, I want to identify emerging Agentic patterns and the agencies building them so that I can secure 3-5 high-value partnerships per quarter.

## Actors

- Opportunity Hunter (Senior PM)

## Preconditions

- The aggregator has processed at least 50 unique articles and threads over the last 48 hours.

## Main Flow

1. The user reviews the 'Market Signals' section of the dashboard.
2. The system highlights a new Agentic pattern appearing across 3 independent sources.
3. The AI identifies 2 boutique AI agencies actively contributing to the pattern's repository.
4. The user clicks 'Extract Partnership Potential' to see a summary of the agencies' work.
5. The user adds the agency to the competitive intelligence tracking list.

## Postconditions

- The user has identified one high-value partnership opportunity.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The summary identifies 3-5 distinct use cases from the aggregated technical data.
- The summary distinguishes between 'Marketing Claims' and 'Developer Validated Utility'.
- The user identifies at least one partnership candidate per week.
