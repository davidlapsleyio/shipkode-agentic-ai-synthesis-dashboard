## Customer FAQ

### How is SignalForge different from my current AI newsletters or RSS feeds?

Unlike standard aggregators or newsletters that provide a list of links, SignalForge uses advanced LLM orchestration to read across sources. It identifies when a Hacker News thread is discussing the same technical flaw mentioned in a GitHub Issue or a TechCrunch article, merging them into a single, cohesive technical briefing.

### Can I trust the benchmarks and sentiment scores provided in the framework comparisons?

For our 'Compare Frameworks' feature, our engine specifically looks for 'friction signals'—developer complaints about latency, state management, or token costs—and 'adoption signals' like repo stars and production deployment mentions. This gives you a 'Developer Sentiment Score' that reflects reality, not marketing.

### I already deal with AI fatigue; won't this just be another dashboard to check?

SignalForge is designed for high-density information environments. We provide a 'Executive Summary' mode that reduces 100+ articles into a 5-minute read, highlighting only market-moving events and new technical patterns, effectively curing 'AI fatigue'.

## Internal FAQ

### How does the system handle de-duplication across text-heavy sources like Hacker News?

We utilize a multi-stage pipeline: first, a high-throughput vector embedding model clusters similar topics; second, a reasoning model (GPT-4o or Claude 3.5) performs the cross-source qualitative synthesis. This minimizes API costs while maintaining high analytical depth.

### What is our strategy for sourcing content from paywalled or 'walled garden' community forums?

We prioritize data sources that have publicly accessible APIs or RSS feeds. For gated content or specialized newsletters, we are exploring partnership agreements to ensure compliance with Terms of Service while providing value to our mutual users.

### What is the monetization strategy and how will we measure product-market fit?

Phase 1 targets the Individual Pro ($49/mo) and Small Team ($199/mo) tiers. Success is measured by 'Research Time Saved' (Target: 70% reduction) and 'Monthly Active Pipeline Adds' for PM users.
